# Current NHS App services

A list of things in the NHS App: 

- NHS login
- Onboarding
- Messaging and push notifications
- Prescriptions
  - Request a repeat prescription
  - Request an emergency prescription
  - Check the progress of prescriptions
  - Medicine record
  - View or change your nominated pharmacy
- Appointments
  - Check for available GP appointments
  - Manage GP appointments
  - Appointment notes and other updates
  - Contact your GP with a question
  - Contact your GP for a fit note
  - Contact your GP for a document
  - Hospital and specialist appointments
  - Referrals
  - Waiting lists
  - Patient-initiated follow-up
- Test results
  - GP-ordered test results
  - Hospital-ordered test results
- Vaccinations
  - Book or cancel a free NHS flue vaccination at a pharmacy
  - Your vaccinations
  - COVID-19 vaccinations
- Health conditions
  - Allergies and adverse reactions
  - All health conditions
- Documents
  - GP documents
  - Hospital and specialist documents and questionnaires
  - Useful links from your health team
- NHS information and support
  - Check your symptoms using NHS 111 online
  - Health A to Z
  - Find NHS services near you
  - Sign up for medical research
- Give feedback about the NHS App
- Contact us (get technical support)
- View your NHS number
- Manage services for someone else (request access, give access, switch profiles)
- Personal details
  - View your current GP surgery
  - Register with a GP
  - Organ donation decision
  - Health data sharing decision
  - Take part in health research
  - Record your symptoms
  - Choose which reocrds your health team can see
  - Manage your health care plans
  - View your universal care plan
- App settings
  - Face ID and Tuch ID
  - Contact and login details
  - Notifications
  - Cookies
- Privacy and legal policies

For more information, you can find the public list of NHS App features [here](https://digital.nhs.uk/services/nhs-app/nhs-app-features).
