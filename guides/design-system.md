# NHS App design system

The NHS App design system is a small extension of the NHS Design System. It includes components and patterns that are specific to the NHS App, and that are not currently included in the NHS Design System. The resources worth knowing about are: 

- [Published guidance](https://design-system.nhsapp.service.nhs.uk/)
- [The codebase](https://github.com/nhsuk/nhsapp-frontend)
- [The issues board](https://github.com/orgs/nhsuk/projects/8)
- [Design history posts](https://design-history.nhsapp.service.nhs.uk/design-system)

Right now, the NHS App design system is web-based, however in the medium-term future this is likely to change to native components and patterns focussed on iOS and Android. We do not yet have a clear plan for what this means for the web-based design system, but we will update this page when we do.
